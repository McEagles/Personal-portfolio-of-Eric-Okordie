BIO

Eric Okordie is an aspiring Data Analyst and passionate problem-solver from Ghana with a background in Information Technology. He holds a BSc in IT from the University of Energy and Natural Resources and has hands-on experience in auditing, data entry, and ICT instruction. But what truly sets Eric apart is his mission: to bridge the digital literacy gap in underserved communities across Ghana. Having taught ICT during his national service at Berekum Senior High School, Eric witnessed firsthand how students' lack of digital skills limited their potential. This experience fueled his commitment not only to master data and cloud technologies, but also to utilise them as tools for educational reform. Eric’s chosen specialisation in Advanced Computer Science and Data Analytics reflects his drive to analyse real-world problems and develop digital solutions that create access and opportunity. Whether it’s building data models to optimise learning platforms or using cloud technologies to scale digital tools, his work is rooted in one goal: empowering Ghanaian youth to thrive in a tech-driven world. In his free time, Eric enjoys exploring new datasets and mentoring others. He’s also known among family and friends as the go-to tech guy, although he once spent 30 minutes trying to fix a projector only to realise it wasn’t plugged in. Eric believes that success isn’t just about what you build, but who you build it for, and he’s building for impact.
